Folds as operations

In Quantum Origami, folds act like operators on a state space. A sequence of folds becomes a geometric circuit.

  • Each crease defines a reflection or rotation.
  • Layering folds encodes compositions.
  • Sequences behave like gates.

Discrete vs continuous

Origami is discrete. Quantum mechanics is continuous. Their tension is where the interesting math lives.
